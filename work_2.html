ARISE

Why

Autism can significantly impact a person's social and interactive skills, often causing challenges for both the individual and their family. Early intervention is crucial to mitigate these difficulties.

What

An AR card game with music therapy elements designed for autistic children and their therapists. The users can play music, exchange cards and explore themed narratives. They play the melodies of the cards to save them, and fix their virtual space with the collected cards. Variety promotes interaction in autism therapy and trading among autistic players.

How

Worked as a team of four, we read through a lot of literature on serious game, music therapy, and autism, and conducted an interview with an autistic individual to understand their needs. We then brainstormed and designed the concept and made a demo video with Unity.
