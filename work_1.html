GIRRAFE KINGDOM

In this project for the module Studio Art Science at Universit√© Paris-Saclay, we worked as a team of 4 to create an AR visual novel with Unity 3D around the theme "more than human".

Background: In this imaginary world, giraffes are the dominant species and humans live under their threats. Giraffes own houses, technologies, and food, and might poach humans with guns. Of course, there are also kind giraffes that would help humans. The player will play a human who needs to try their best to find a way to survive.

Objective: The purpose of this work is to put players in the position of animals, and raise their awareness of the experiences of animals living under the threats of climate change and human activities.

To play our game, you will need an Android phone and some cards.
