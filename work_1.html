GIRRAFE KINGDOM

Why

To put players in the position of animals, and raise their awareness of the experiences of animals living under the threats of climate change and human activities.

What

An AR visual novel. In this imaginary world, giraffes are the dominant species and humans live under their threats. Giraffes own houses, technologies, and food, and might poach humans with guns. There are also kind giraffes that would help humans. The player will play a human who needs to try their best to find a way to survive.

How

Worked as a team of four, we brainstormed, prototyped with pen and paper, and iterated our idea based on feedbacks from peers. Next, we wrote the script and made the branching paths in Canvas. We then created the game with Unity, tested it and finally showcased it on an event.
