What we expect

We expect our volunteers to engage with at least one of the following activities:

•  Provide help to the older adults in your local community.

•  Helo organize and manage our virtual and in-person events.

•  Raise people's awareness of the living conditions of older adults, both on citizen media and in your real life.

•  Sign joint letters that drive policy changes.
