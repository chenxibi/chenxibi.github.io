Co-creating Our Futures: Participatory design workshop on communication technologies

 

In-person, 14th August, 3:00 – 5:00pm

Would you like to get involved in product design processes, through practical activities, as one of the stakeholders to help designers and researchers discover your unmet needs? There's a chance we could make it now!

Co-creating Our Futures is a series of participatory design workshops which enable non-designers to have their voices in the design process of a product, service, or experience to co-design solutions to a variety of problems.

Our workshop for this month is on designing communication technologies for and with older adults. If you are 65 or older, have interests in or needs for communication technologies, and are willing to take an active role in our discussions, please do not hesitate to come!

Opening Up: Share your digital experiences

 

In-person, 30th August, 4:00 – 6:00pm

This event will feature older adults' experiences with the internet. We will invite several seniors who are active internet users to give a talk to the older adults in our community about how they learned to use the internet and how positive experiences with the internet have improved their lives. Then, participants will have the chance to ask questions and talk about their concerns on technologies and the internet. We will also have a practical session in which we stimulate scenarios of real-life situations from shopping online to calling an Uber.

By organizing this event we aim to help seniors overcome their fears of the internet and their inconfidence in their digital skills.
