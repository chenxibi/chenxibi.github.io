EcoMeter

Why

To help users remain aware of the environmental impact of their everyday electricity consumpation.

What

A tool that calculates the user's carbon footprint based on the data of their electricity bill.

How

Worked as a team of four, we conducted desk and user research to understand the needs of users. Then, we made lo-fi prototypes and conducted user testing to evaluate its usability. Finally, we created high-fi prototypes.



Type of work
UI/UX Design

My responsibilities
Desk research, user research, low-fi prototyping, user testing, high-fi prototyping.
